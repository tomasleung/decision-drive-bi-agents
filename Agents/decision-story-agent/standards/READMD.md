# Standards

## Decision Story Agent

# Purpose

This folder contains the governing standards used by the Decision Story Agent.

Standards define:

- What Good Looks Like
- Required Design Principles
- Required Validation Rules
- Required Traceability Rules
- Required Decision-Driven Design Rules

The standards ensure all Decision Story artifacts are:

- Consistent
- Deterministic
- Governed
- Repeatable
- Traceable
- Decision Driven

across all projects.

---

# Why Standards Exist

The Decision Story Agent transforms:

Business Requirements
↓
Decision Design
↓
Story Design
↓
Decision Story Contract

Without standards, reports become:

Data Driven
↓
Visual Driven
↓
Dashboard Driven
↓
Technology Driven

instead of:

Decision Driven
↓
Question Driven
↓
Action Driven
