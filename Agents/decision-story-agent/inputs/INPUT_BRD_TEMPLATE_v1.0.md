A Business Requirements Document succeeds when:

Every Business Goal
supports a Decision

Every Decision
supports Business Questions

Every Business Question
supports a Signal

Every Signal
supports a KPI or Action

Every Action
supports a Business Outcome

Every Requirement
supports Decision Making

REPORT_STORY
